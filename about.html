About Phendit Disability & Home Care

Home / About Us

Committed to dignity, safety, and wellbeing.

At Phendit Disability & Home Care, we support individuals and families by providing high-quality disability and home care services that promote independence, safety, and wellbeing.

Our services are delivered with respect, professionalism, and a strong commitment to participant choice and control. We believe quality care starts with listening—then building support around what matters most to you.

"Our approach is simple: listen first, personalise support, and walk alongside you."
