Purpose of the ICAN Support Program

  • Short-term care when NDIS funding is temporarily insufficient or delayed.
  • Essential Support to maintain health, safety, and wellbeing.
  • Ethical solution with strong safeguarding and regulatory compliance.

ICAN stands for Inclusive Care & Access Network — because every need deserves impactful touch.

ICAN Support
Right for Me?

For individuals already in the NDIS, losing support shortfalls, and needing short-term assistance — ICAN may be of benefit.

Eligibility Criteria for ICAN Support

ICAN eligibility is carefully limited to the following scenarios to ensure it is ethical, needs-based, and aligned with regulatory requirements.

Funding Delay

Your NDIS plan approval is significantly delayed or pending review.

Learn more >

Funding Gap

You are experiencing a temporary pause in existing funding.

Learn more >

Transition Period

You are new to the NDIS and in the onboarding phase.

Learn more >

Unexpected Need

You have an unexpected situation requiring immediate support.
