Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our highest priorities.

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Commitment to Privacy

Phendit Disability & Home Care is committed to protecting the privacy of personal information we collect and hold about individuals. We comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

To provide NDIS and home care services, we may collect:

  • Personal details (Name, DOB, Contact details).
  • Health and disability information (Medical history, support plans).
  • NDIS Plan details and funding information.
  • Emergency contact and next of kin details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We collect personal information primarily to:
• Provide safe and appropriate care services.
• Develop and review your Service Agreement and Support Plan.
• Communicate with you, your family, or advocates.
• Process payments and claims (e.g., via the NDIS portal).
• Comply with legal and regulatory obligations (NDIS Commission).

4. Disclosure of Information

We generally do not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent, unless required by law. Exceptions include:
• Sharing information with other health professionals involved in your care.
• Reporting to the NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission if an incident occurs.
• Emergency situations where safety is at risk.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorized access, and modification. We store records securely in compliance with NDIS Practice Standards.

6. Access and Correction

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you. If you believe any information is incorrect, incomplete, or out of date, please contact us to have it corrected.
